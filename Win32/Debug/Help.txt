Welcome to Ecoverse

Ecoverse is a program that aims to promote environmental awareness and provide information on how to save our planet. You are currently on the Ecoverse Home Page; here you can read information on how to reuse, reduce and recycle, join our exciting events and workshops, donate to our sponsored fundraisers and more!

Joining our events and donating to fundraisers also earns you points! You can use these points to buy some wonderful goodies from our partnered shop, Eco Element Co.

Point System:
Event - 10 points
Hosting an Event (for Admins only) - 15 points
Donation - Amount of money / 2 (i.e. 1 point per R2)

We hope this has been of great help to you and that you have as much fun as us through our journey to creating a better world!
